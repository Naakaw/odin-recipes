LASAGNA

Lasagna recipe

This is how you make a great lasagna. This recipe was passed down from generation to generation

The main ingridient of the lasagna is the tomato and pasta so it has to be good check if it is.

Here are all the ingridients:

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta in a salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. In a seperate pan, on a medium-high heat add two tablespoons of butter, wait until melted, then add a tablespoon of flour and mix until dissolved. Add two cups of milk and bring to a boil. When the sauce thickens add salt and pepper and turn the heat off.
  3. On a medium-high heat place a pan with a little bit of olive oil. Give it some time to heat up and then add the beef. Stir occasionaly until the meat is cooked.
  4. Assembly the lasagna by placing a layer of pasta, followed by a layer of beef, beshamel sauce and tomato sauce. Repeat until out of ingridients.
  5. Grate some cheese on top
  6. Place in the oven on 270 degrees for thirty minutes.
